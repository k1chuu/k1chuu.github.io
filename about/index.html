About k1chuu

I am a security researcher focused on low-level exploitation, kernel security, and vulnerability discovery. This blog serves as a repository for documenting deep dives into research, technical analysis, and the underlying systems internals.

🔍 Research Focus

  • Heap exploitation & memory corruption primitives
  • Linux kernel attack surfaces & privilege escalation
  • Fuzzing methodologies & binary analysis automation
  • CTF challenge development & solution analysis

🛠️ Tooling & Stack

Most research is conducted using: GDB/Pwndbg, QEMU, AFL++, and custom Python/C tooling. The blog is statically generated for speed & simplicity.
